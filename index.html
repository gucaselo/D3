Correlations Discovered Between multiple categories

Using this interactive Scatter Plot, you can choose what categories you want to compare and see how they relate to each other. In the X axis you can choose from Poverty, Age or Household Income and in the Yaxis you can choose either Healthcare, Smokes or Obesity. One additional feature you can find is that by hovering over a circle you can get more detail about their relationship.

Data source:
US Census
